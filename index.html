Action Gameplay

Leap through vividly hand-crafted mountains, forests, deserts and shores in this pixel art action platformer. Face against tricky foes in a unique combat system that rewards quick thinking with colourfully acrobatic action abilities. Leap, roll, slash and parry to capture enemy powers and use them to your advantage in combat and traversal!

Sweeping Story

Encounter a cast of charming characters in a world with rich lore and intrigue as your path crosses with theirs. The tale of Insignia takes place in a fantasy world where humans are just one of many races of animals with language, history and culture. When the threat of war is on the horizon, can fingers and claws come together or are they destined to tear each other apart?

Curious Crafting

Master your crafting skills to forge new items and abilities, unlocking secrets and opening new paths through the world. Discover forgotten Crucibles in the far corners of the kingdom to upgrade your sword's capabilities. It is said that in the beginning the unseen ones wrought nature with mere words. Could learning their language reveal the power to reforge nature itself?

Dark Dungeons

Delve into vast and challenging dungeons with tricky puzzles, treacherous foes and spectacular boss fights. Put everything you have learned to the test and claim the pieces of Insignia before they fall into the hands of the enemy!

The story so far...

Kiama is a world not unlike ours, though inhabited by many animal civilisations with their own culture and customs. The peoples of the world were first brought together in the light of the Insignia, a prismatic force that shapes the world in its words they called Ensigns.

Centuries ago, cataclysmic wars between the Humen and Hunden were fought for the control of the Insignia. After a desperate final measure, the victorious kingdom of men vowed to abandon their use of the power, leaving a scarred capitol city to build new townships across the country.

Now, with the wartime arts all but forgotten, a shadow returns to the kingdom. Armin’s story begins with the disappearance of his father, a renowned blacksmith. With nothing but a cryptic journal to lead the way, Armin must follow Magnus’ path to revive an ancient power and prevent a new war from destroying everything he holds dear.

What kind of game is Insignia?

Insignia is an action-adventure game featuring exploration-based progression, environmental puzzles and combat mechanics. Players navigate the world unlocking new abilities and learning the story of our beloved protagonist, Armin.

If you enjoy games that reward curiosity, Insignia offers a modern take on classic adventure games.

Does the game have multiplayer or online features?

Insignia is a single player experience that does not require an internet connection to play.

How long will the game be?

The main story spans five chapters with 12-20 hours of content, plus additional exploration and optional challenges for completionist players.

When will Insignia be released?

Development is actively ongoing. Follow AdamCYounis on social media or join the development streams on Twitch for the latest progress updates!

Which platforms will Insignia be available on?

Insignia is being developed for PC (Steam) and Nintendo Switch, with potential additional platforms to be announced.

Does the game have controller support?

Yes, Insignia has full gamepad support on PC.

Which languages will the game support?

Multiple languages are planned. Specific languages will be announced closer to release.

Who is developing Insignia?

Insignia began as a solo project by Adam Younis in 2017. As of 2024 Insignia is being developed by a small team of artists and developers.

Can I follow the development process?

Yes! Insignia's development streams happen most weekdays on Twitch, with vlods and vlogs available on YouTube @AdamCYounis.
