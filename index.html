What platforms will the game be available on?

♦

The game will initially launch on PC via Steam, with additional platforms to be announced later. We're committed to bringing Insignia to as many players as possible.

Stay tuned to our social media channels for the latest platform announcements and release updates.

Will there be multiplayer features?

♦

Insignia is primarily designed as a single-player adventure focusing on Armin's personal journey and story.

However, we're exploring potential cooperative features that could enhance the experience while maintaining the narrative focus.

How long is the gameplay experience?

♦

We're designing Insignia to provide a substantial adventure that respects players' time while delivering a complete and satisfying story.

The exact playtime will depend on your exploration style and how deeply you engage with the world's secrets and side content.

Will there be accessibility options?

♦

Accessibility is important to us, and we're committed to making Insignia enjoyable for players with different needs and preferences.

We'll share more details about specific accessibility features as we get closer to release.

Can I support the game's development?

♦

The best way to support Insignia right now is to wishlist the game on Steam and follow our social media channels to stay updated.

Sharing the game with friends who might enjoy this type of adventure also helps us tremendously!
