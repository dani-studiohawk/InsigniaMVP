Frequently Asked Questions

Insignia is an action-adventure game featuring exploration-based progression, environmental puzzles and combat mechanics. Players navigate the world unlocking new abilities and learning the story of our beloved protagonist, Armin.

If you enjoy games that reward curiosity, Insignia offers a modern take on classic adventure games.

Insignia is a single player experience that does not require an internet connection to play.

The main story spans five chapters with 12-20 hours of content, plus additional exploration and optional challenges for completionist players.

Development is actively ongoing. Follow AdamCYounis on social media or join the development streams on Twitch for the latest progress updates!

Insignia is being developed for PC (Steam) and Nintendo Switch, with potential additional platforms to be announced.

Yes, Insignia has full gamepad support on PC.

Multiple languages are planned. Specific languages will be announced closer to release.

Insignia began as a solo project by Adam Younis in 2017. As of 2024 Insignia is being developed by a small team of artists and developers.

Yes! Insignia's development streams happen most weekdays on Twitch, with vlods and vlogs available on YouTube @AdamCYounis.
